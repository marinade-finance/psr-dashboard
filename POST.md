# Phase III: Validator Stake Cap Increase

## Discord Post

📢 **Validator Stake Cap Increasing — Step 1 Live at Epoch 924**

As part of MIP-19, we're raising the maximum stake a single validator
can obtain from 4% to up to 15% of TVL. This is being rolled out
gradually to ensure smooth transition.

**Step 1 (epoch 924):** Cap raised from 4% → 7% of TVL.
**Step 2:** Cap raised to 15%, after observing auction health for at
least 14 days.

Top-performing validators now have room to acquire more stake by
adjusting their `maxStakeWanted` and offering competitive bids. Stake
allocation remains fully determined by validator bids, bonds, and
automated optimization — the auction works exactly as before, just
with higher headroom.

We'll share updates before proceeding to step 2.
